Royal Blood are an English rock duo formed in Brighton in 2011, consisting of Mike Kerr (vocals, bass) and Ben Thatcher (drums). Their sound is anchored in Kerr's unique bass playing technique, in which he uses various effects pedals and multiple amps to make his electric bass sound like a standard electric guitar and a bass guitar at the same time.[5] Their self-titled debut album was released in August 2014. Their second album How Did We Get So Dark? followed in June 2017.
